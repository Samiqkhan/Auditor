Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes and across various industries, including startups, small and medium enterprises, established companies, and multinational corporations. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each client, regardless of their size or sector.

How long does the GST registration process take?

The GST registration process typically takes 3-7 working days from the date of application submission, provided all required documents are in order. Our team ensures that the application is prepared correctly and follows up with the tax authorities to expedite the process.

What documents are required for income tax return filing?

The documents required for income tax return filing include PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16 (for salaried individuals), bank statements, investment proofs, property documents (if applicable), and any other income or deduction-related documents. The specific requirements may vary based on the type of taxpayer and the nature of income.

What are the eligibility criteria for your internship programs?

Our internship programs are open to students pursuing degrees in accounting, finance, economics, or related fields. Candidates should have a strong academic background, basic knowledge of accounting principles, and a keen interest in learning practical aspects of the profession. Both undergraduate and postgraduate students are welcome to apply.

How can I determine which insurance policy is right for me?

Determining the right insurance policy depends on various factors including your age, health condition, financial situation, family responsibilities, and future goals. Our insurance experts conduct a thorough assessment of your needs and risk profile to recommend policies that provide optimal coverage at competitive premiums. We help you compare different options and make an informed decision.
